About MyBank

MyBank is a pioneering financial institution founded and owned by Maryan Abdi Hussein. Located in the heart of Mogadishu, Somalia, our bank was officially established in 2025 with a mission to provide secure, reliable, and customer-focused banking services. At MyBank, we are committed to innovation, transparency, and excellence, aiming to empower individuals and businesses through smart financial solutions.

Our team of dedicated professionals works tirelessly to offer modern banking services, including deposits, withdrawals, account management, and transaction tracking. We prioritize the safety of your money while ensuring that your banking experience is seamless and user-friendly. MyBank is not just a financial institution; it is a vision for a stronger economic future in Somalia, helping our customers achieve their financial goals with confidence and trust.

Whether you are an individual seeking personal banking solutions or a business looking for efficient financial services, MyBank is your trusted partner. We believe in building lasting relationships with our clients, guided by integrity, professionalism, and innovation. Welcome to MyBank – your gateway to smart, secure, and reliable banking in Somalia.
